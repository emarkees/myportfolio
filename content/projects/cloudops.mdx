## 01 / Executive Summary

CloudOps Sentinel is a unified SRE pane of glass with automated PagerDuty escalations and incident runbooks. It aggregates signals from multiple sources and predicts outages before they impact customers.

Built primarily with Node.js and deployed via Terraform.

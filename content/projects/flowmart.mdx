## 01 / Executive Summary

FlowMart is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** built to modernize wholesale and last-mile commerce within the **RCCG Redemption Camp (Redemption City)** ecosystem. Originally developed during the **RCCG Youth and Young Adults (YAYA) Global — Kingdom Hack 3.0** hackathon under the Commerce track, FlowMart has since evolved into an ongoing production platform.

The platform serves as a **unified market infrastructure** connecting local buyers, small vendors, and independent delivery couriers under one system. Users can seamlessly switch between three distinct personas without separate accounts:

- **Buyer Mode** — Browse local shops, purchase groceries & catered food, and track deliveries in real time.
- **Vendor Mode** — List products, open a digital storefront, manage inventory, and receive direct payments.
- **Rider Mode** — Pick up marketplace deliveries, use QR-scan verification for orders, and coordinate routing via GPS.

## 02 / Key Features

- **Three-in-One Role Switching** — Buyers, vendors, and riders operate within a single app, switching modes instantly without separate accounts.
- **Real-Time Rider Tracking** — Leaflet GPS maps with Socket.io + Redis Pub/Sub deliver sub-5-second rider location updates.
- **Escrow-Protected Payments** — Funds are held securely via Paystack until delivery is confirmed by QR-scan verification.
- **Neighbourhood Classifieds** — A local listings section for community buying and selling within Redemption City.
- **Offline-First PWA** — Service worker architecture ensures core functionality even with intermittent connectivity.

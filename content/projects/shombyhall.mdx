## 01 / Executive Summary

Shomby Banqueting Hall is a luxury event venue located in Manchester, UK, specializing in weddings, corporate events, and private parties. I developed their digital storefront and booking platform.

## 02 / Architecture & Implementation

The platform was built with a modern stack focusing on performance and user experience. Visitors can explore flexible capacity options, custom decor packages, and submit booking inquiries seamlessly.

Key features include:
* **Responsive Design:** Ensuring a premium feel across mobile and desktop.
* **Booking System:** Streamlined reservation forms for rapid turnaround.
* **SEO Optimization:** Implemented SEO best practices to improve local search visibility for Manchester venues.
